About Bangalore

Bengaluru (formerly Bangalore), the capital of Karnataka, is India's premier IT hub and fastest-growing metropolis, often called the "Silicon Valley of India". Located on the Deccan Plateau, it is known for its pleasant year-round climate, diverse culture, and rapid urbanization. With a population exceeding 10 million, the city blends modern tech hubs with historical sites.

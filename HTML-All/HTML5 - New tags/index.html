About Bangalore

Bangalore, the capital of Karnataka, is known as the Silicon Valley of India due to its thriving IT industry and tech startups. The city offers a perfect blend of modern infrastructure and historic landmarks, including the iconic Vidhana Soudha and lush Cubbon Park. With its pleasant climate, cosmopolitan culture, and vibrant nightlife, Bangalore attracts professionals and tourists from around the world.
